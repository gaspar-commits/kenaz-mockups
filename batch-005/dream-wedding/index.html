Why Costa Blanca

There is a reason couples from across Europe choose this stretch of Mediterranean coast. It offers what no amount of decoration can manufacture.

320 Days of Sun

The Costa Blanca enjoys one of the most reliable climates in Europe. Your outdoor ceremony is not a gamble. It is practically a guarantee.

Dramatic Landscapes

From the iconic Peñón de Ifach to hidden coves and terraced vineyards, every backdrop is a masterpiece that no decorator could create.
